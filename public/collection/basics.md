# Computer Science

Q: What is the time complexity of binary search?
A: $O(\log n)$ - halves the search space each iteration.

C: A [stack] uses Last-In-First-Out (LIFO), while a [queue] uses First-In-First-Out (FIFO).

# Economics

Q: What is the law of supply and demand?
A: When demand increases while supply stays constant, price rises. When supply increases while demand stays constant, price falls.

C: [Opportunity cost] is the value of the next-best alternative you give up when making a choice.
