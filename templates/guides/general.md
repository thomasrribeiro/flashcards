# Compatibility notice: universal authoring guidance moved

This former long-form guide is intentionally retained as a compatibility path
for older decks and links. Do not load it as a separate source of authoring
rules.

Use, in order:

1. [CARD_STANDARD.md](CARD_STANDARD.md) for the testable acceptance rules;
2. [AUTHORING_PLAYBOOK.md](AUTHORING_PLAYBOOK.md) for curriculum, learning, and
   audit decisions;
3. the applicable subject guide for domain-specific judgment.

The old guide mixed parser rules, learning advice, examples, subject-specific
material, and obsolete generation instructions. Its valid guidance has been
consolidated into the files above.
